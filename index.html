CACAO

CACAO is a decentralized music platform. Creating a new music industry economy.

Enabling artists to connect directly with their audience. Allowing them to upload and take control over their music without the need of intermediaries.

CACAO reminds us of that time before european economies were introduced to America. Remembering that pre-Columbian cultures used CACAO seeds as currency.

With this we must always bear in mind that as long as there is coordination and the agreeing upon value we are enabled to set ourselves forth towards the building of new economies.

We know of music being an artistic means of expression. We have been accustomed to consume music as a free good thanks to business models where music and musicians were utilized as a source of entertainment but mostly as attention funnels concentrating the audience's attention enabling businesses to insert ads of their selling products. In archaic economies such as these artists where the less benefited followed by their audience fellows.

CACAO stands for regaining the value of music. Exploring tools such as IPFS and the Ethereum Blockchain enabling us to experiment with decentralized solutions for storage and retrieval where both users and artists may become active parties of the economy.

Exploring tools such as the ERC-721 standard offering us the opportunity to experiment with concepts such as uniqueness and transparent and equitative royalties distribution.

As we enter to the midst of year 2021 we have seen a rise in the interest of NFT technology. We have seen the pitching of solutions for the arts and music fair propagation. But we have seen too platforms being seduced by the old ways i.e. endorsing already famous actors of the old archaic economies. Leaving the independent artists in the same place as where they started off once again.
